Pan Fried Egg

Eggs have always been my go-to cheap convenience food because they are easy, quick, cheap, and endlessly versatile.

Learn how to pan fry one yourself with this quick and easy guide.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
    •

    Steps

    1. Heat butter in the pan.
    2. Crack egg into the pan.
    3. Cook on medium-high until the egg is slightly browned on the bottom.
    4. Flip, then cook a minute or so longer.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Enjoy!
