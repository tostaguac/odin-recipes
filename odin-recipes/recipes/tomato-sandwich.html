Farm-Fresh Tomato Sandwich

Given how easy a tomato sandwich is, maybe the next time I find myself in a field surrounded by tomato vines.
I'll be ready not just with salt, but some bread and mayo as well.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Bread
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Mayonaise
    •

    Steps

    1. Melt butter in pan, toast bread until browned.
    2. Slather toast with mayonaise.
    3. Apply tomato to sandwich, season with salt to taste.
    4. Enjoy!
